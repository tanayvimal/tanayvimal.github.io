About

I am Tanay Vimal, a Mechanical Engineering student at Amrita School of Engineering, Bangalore, with a strong passion for automobiles, manufacturing systems, and mechanical design. My interests span engineering graphics, 3D modeling, product development, and the study of automotive technologies, where I enjoy understanding how engineering principles are applied to create efficient and reliable systems.

I am particularly interested in using CAD tools and technical design methodologies to transform ideas into practical solutions. Constantly exploring new technologies and design approaches, I aim to deepen my knowledge of manufacturing processes and vehicle engineering while developing the skills needed to tackle real-world engineering challenges.

My long-term goal is to contribute to the automotive industry through innovative design, manufacturing excellence, and process optimization, helping create smarter and more efficient engineering solutions for the future.
